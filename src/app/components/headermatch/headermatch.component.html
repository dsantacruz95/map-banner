What's this?

I developed this webpage to enhance the competitive experience in Adrenaline Gamer and Half-Life Deathmatch by streamlining the map selection process. Players can vote to eliminate maps until only one remains, ensuring a fair and agreed-upon final map.

This tool speeds up decision-making, reduces disputes, and supports efficient match management. It promotes fair play and strategic planning, aiming to elevate the quality of gameplay in these communities. Ultimately, it creates a more enjoyable and professional tournament experience.
